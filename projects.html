Title: Designing Superpixel-Based Multi-Resolution Model for Enhanced Object Delineation

This work introduces a novel superpixel-based segmentation framework that integrates fuzzy clustering, graph-based segmentation, and wavelet-based multi-resolution refinement. It addresses noise sensitivity and boundary rigidity by leveraging Fuzzy C-Means (FCM), Hierarchical Normalized Cuts (HNCut), and spatial-visual affinity metrics including CIEDE2000, LBP, and HSV histograms.

The model significantly improves object delineation accuracy in low-contrast and texture-rich images, with applications in medical imaging and surveillance.
